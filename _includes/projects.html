The State Islamic University of Sultan Syarif Kasim (UIN Suska) Riau in Arabic is جامعة السلطان شريف قاسم الإسلامية الحكومية رياو and in English is the State Islamic University of Sultan Syarif Kasim Riau is the result of the development / improvement of educational status of the State Islamic Institute (IAIN) Sulthan Syarif Qasim Pekanbaru officially confirmed on January 4, 2005 and inaugurated on February 9, 2005 by the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Dr. H. Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono. Sulthan Syarif Qasim Institute of Islamic Studies (IAIN Susqa) Pekanbaru as the forerunner of UIN Suska Riau, was established on September 19, 1970. IAIN Susqa originally came from several Faculties of Private Islamic Higher Education which was then declared, the Faculty of Tarbiyah Islamic University Riau in Pekanbaru, Sharia Faculty of Islamic University of Riau in Tembilahan, and Faculty of Ushuluddin Grand Mosque of An-Nur Pekanbaru.

IAIN Sultan Syarif Qasim (IAIN Suska) established Information Systems Department in the academic year 2002/2003 in line with the plan to improve the status of IAIN to State Islamic University (UIN) Riau, which has now been implemented. This strategic department will also support the Faculty of Science and Technology and the development of Islamic studies in the third millennium. IAIN Susqa Pekanbaru (currently changed to UIN Suska Riau) as one of the Islamic educational institutions in Riau observed and attempted to answer this challenge by establishing the Department of Information Systems.
